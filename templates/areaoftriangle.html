About Triangle Area

The area of a triangle is calculated using the formula A = ½ × base × height, where the base is any side of the triangle and the height is the perpendicular distance from that base to the opposite vertex.

This calculator uses the basic triangle area formula. Make sure to measure the height perpendicular to the base, not along one of the other sides. Both measurements should be in the same units for accurate results.
