About Circle Area

The area of a circle is calculated using the mathematical formula A = π × r², where π (pi) is approximately 3.14159 and r is the radius of the circle.

This calculator uses the precise value of π to give you accurate results. The radius is the distance from the center of the circle to any point on its edge.
